Free CRB faucet

New here? Grab a tiny bit of CRB to try the wallet and send your first transaction - no rig needed.
One claim per address and per IP every 3 hours. The amount scales with your mining: 0.001 CRB to start, 0.01 after a block, 0.1 past 100 blocks. To claim, your browser mines one real NeuroMorph share - genuine work in our own algorithm that mines toward the faucet's treasury (and now and then strikes a full block for it). That's the anti-bot check: bots have to actually mine for us.
Want more than a drop? Mine CRB - 50 CRB per block, on any CPU.

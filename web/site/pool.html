Mining pool ● LIVE

Steady, low-variance rewards: the pool pays you proportionally to the work you submit, even when network difficulty is high. Point the stock miner at it - nothing else to install.

Mine on the pool

cereblix-miner -addr crb1YOURADDRESS -node https://cereblix.com/pool/api
🇷🇺 RU / CIS - if cereblix.com is slow or blocked, mine through our Moscow relay (same pool, no Cloudflare): cereblix-miner -addr crb1YOURADDRESS -node https://ru.cereblix.com/pool/api
Your miner logs "share accepted" - those are shares (proofs of work at an easier target), not full blocks. Rewards arrive automatically as pool payouts once your balance crosses the minimum. Each share is cryptographically bound to your address (per-miner extranonce), so no one can claim your work.
⏳ Payouts take ~100 blocks (about 1.5–2 hours) to start. A block reward only becomes spendable after 100 confirmations (coinbase maturity) - a standard safeguard so the pool never pays out coins from a block that could still be reversed by a chain reorganization. Your earnings accrue immediately and are paid as soon as they mature and cross the minimum payout.
